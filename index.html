Hardware and Software in Food Production

In part A we will be lisitng software and hardware technologies and gadgest used in food production

Hardware

Thermocouple (RTD)

provides temperature input for the operation of a retort to tell if the product is in the recommended temperature

Microprocessors

are used for controlling a steam valve, maintaining pH, etc.

Mainframe computers

are used to coordinate an entire plant, such as environment, production, records, and inventory.

Software

AIFS

will help inform decisions about cleaning to improve sanitation and reduce the spread of pathogens.

NIR

will sort poor-quality fruits and veggies from the good ones.

AI cameras
